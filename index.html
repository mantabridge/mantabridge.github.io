How it works

Manta Bridge in three steps

Open the app, choose the route, and confirm from your wallet. The flow is built for people who want Manta Pacific access without handing custody to an exchange.

1

Connect your wallet

Start from the official Pacific bridge flow or open this app, then connect a Web3 wallet such as MetaMask.

2

Select Ethereum, Manta Pacific or another supported path, then choose ETH, MANTA or the asset available for that route.

3

Review and confirm

Check the live quote, estimated processing and wallet approval details, then sign only when the route looks right.

FAQ

Manta Bridge questions

01What is Manta Bridge?

Manta Bridge is a non-custodial bridge and swap entry point for moving crypto assets toward Manta Pacific and related Manta routes. It keeps the important checks in front of you: source chain, destination chain, asset, quote, time and wallet approval.

02Which chains and assets can I use?

Common routes include Ethereum and Manta Pacific, with ETH, MANTA and route-specific assets shown inside the app. For a broader view of bridge markets, compare public bridge activity and check our supported chains page.

03How long does Manta bridging take?

Timing depends on the route, chain congestion, finality and wallet confirmations. The app shows the current estimate before you confirm, and Ethereum bridging guidance is useful background for how cross-chain transfers behave.

04What does it cost?

You pay the network and route costs shown at execution time. Manta Bridge avoids made-up preview prices; the live quote and wallet transaction details are where the real cost appears.

05Is Manta Bridge safe to use?

Use the same discipline you would for any DeFi bridge: verify the URL, inspect wallet prompts, understand bridge risk, and review independent L2 risk profiles before moving meaningful funds.
