What is Manta Bridge?

Manta Bridge usually refers to bridge flows for Manta Network, especially the official Manta Pacific bridge from Ethereum to Manta Pacific. Manta describes itself as a modular ecosystem for high-performance decentralized applications in its official docs. Use this as an independent guide, not an official app, and verify the domain before connecting a wallet.

How Manta Bridge works

The main Manta Pacific bridge flow starts with Ethereum Mainnet as the source and Manta Pacific Mainnet as the destination. Exact token support, timing, and cost components can change in the live interface.

  1. Start from an official pageUse Manta Network, the official Manta docs, or the Manta Pacific bridge URL, then confirm the domain before connecting a wallet.
  2. Choose direction and assetSelect deposit or withdraw, source network, destination network, token, and amount. Keep the native gas token available on the chain that signs the transaction.
  3. Review approvals and feesCheck token approval, source-chain gas, destination execution or claim gas, and any route-specific bridge or third-party fee shown by the interface.
  4. Track the transferAfter signing, follow the bridge history or block explorer status and do not repeat transactions just because a wallet balance updates slowly.

Fees and security

There is no universal mantabridge fee or arrival time. Costs and risk depend on gas markets, the selected path, token approvals, bridge contracts, and any third-party provider involved.

Cost components

Expect source-chain gas, token approval gas when required, destination gas or claim cost when required, and any route fee displayed before signing. The official Manta Pacific Bridge is the place to review the current quote.

Trust model

Bridge designs vary: some paths rely on native rollup contracts, while third-party routes may use liquidity networks, relayers, or other verification models. Ethereum.org's bridge overview explains why bridge trust assumptions matter.

Network verification

Manta Pacific is an Ethereum L2 network with its own chain settings and explorer. The official network information lists the chain ID, RPC endpoints, explorer links, and ETH gas symbol.

Route notes before bridging

Manta has more than one bridge-related surface, so match the guide you are reading to the network you actually intend to use.

Manta Pacific bridge

The public Pacific bridge interface shows Ethereum Mainnet to Manta Pacific Mainnet for deposits and includes a withdraw tab. Start from pacific-bridge.manta.network or Manta's main site rather than search ads.

Pacific and Atlantic are different

Manta Pacific is the EVM L2, while Manta Atlantic is the Polkadot-based network used for identity and credential use cases. Manta's introduction docs explain both networks.

Third-party bridges

The Manta Pacific bridge page also lists independent third-party providers. Manta says those services are linked for convenience and are not operated by Manta, so read the provider's own docs and use the official page to verify context.

Manta Bridge FAQ

Is mantabridge the official Manta Bridge?

Mantabridge is a search term, not a guarantee that a page is official. The official Manta site links to the Pacific bridge at pacific-bridge.manta.network and Manta's broader app at app.manta.network. Verify the domain before connecting a wallet.

What is Manta Pacific?

Manta Pacific is Manta Network's EVM-compatible Ethereum L2 for scalable decentralized applications. Manta's official introduction describes Pacific as the L2 side of the Manta ecosystem, separate from Manta Atlantic. That distinction matters when choosing a bridge route and wallet.

What fees apply when using mantabridge?

Fees are components, not one fixed number: source-chain gas, token approval gas when needed, destination gas or claim cost when required, and any bridge or third-party provider fee shown before signing. Review the current quote in the official Manta Pacific bridge interface.

Is Manta Bridge safe?

Every bridge has risk. Users should account for smart-contract risk, wrong-network transfers, wallet approvals, phishing, route downtime, and third-party provider assumptions. Ethereum.org's bridge overview explains common bridge trust models and risks in neutral terms.

Can I bridge MANTA between Manta Pacific and Manta Atlantic?

Manta docs include instructions for bridging MANTA between Manta Pacific and Manta Atlantic, including using both an Atlantic wallet and a Pacific wallet in the flow. Read the current native bridge guide before starting.

How do I verify a Manta Pacific transaction?

Use the official Manta Pacific explorer link from Manta's network information page, then search by transaction hash or address. The network information docs list explorer URLs, RPC endpoints, chain ID, and other details needed to confirm you are on the right network.
