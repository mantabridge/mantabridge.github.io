What is Manta Bridge?

Manta Bridge is the Manta Network bridge interface for moving supported ERC-20 assets from Ethereum Mainnet to Manta Pacific, the Ethereum L2 in the Manta ecosystem; review the official bridge guide before signing.

Manta Bridge supported assets and networks

Manta Bridge usually refers to the official Manta Pacific bridge for moving assets between Ethereum mainnet and Manta Pacific. The visible default route on the bridge is Ethereum Mainnet to Manta Pacific Mainnet with ETH, and the page also points users to third-party bridges for additional assets or routes. Manta Network has separate product history around Manta Atlantic, but Manta's own site warns that Manta Atlantic will sunset on August 1, 2026. Users should distinguish Manta Pacific deposits from Atlantic migration or staking pages before transferring funds.

Manta Bridge fees and gas costs

Manta Bridge costs are mainly Ethereum gas, Manta Pacific transaction costs, and any route-specific third-party bridge fee if the user leaves the official bridge flow. The official bridge page warns users to double-check Ethereum gas fees, which can dominate the cost for small deposits. A cheap bridge quote can still become expensive if the source-chain transaction is costly or if the user needs an additional swap after arrival. The best comparison is the total received asset on Manta Pacific after gas, service fees, and slippage.

How to bridge ETH to Manta Pacific

To bridge ETH to Manta Pacific, connect an EVM wallet, choose Ethereum Mainnet as the source, select Manta Pacific Mainnet as the destination, enter the ETH amount, review the expected received amount, and approve the transaction. The bridge page also asks users to add the Manta Pacific chain to MetaMask before bridging. After submission, the Ethereum transaction must confirm before funds appear on Manta Pacific. Users should avoid sending directly to bridge contracts or copying instructions from unofficial guides.

Manta Bridge withdrawal from Manta Pacific to Ethereum

Withdrawals from Manta Pacific to Ethereum can involve different timing and costs than deposits. Users should check the official bridge interface for the current withdrawal path, destination asset, and claim requirements because rollup bridges and third-party bridges do not behave identically. If a third-party bridge is used, the provider's liquidity and message-passing design control timing. For large withdrawals, users should compare the official bridge with listed providers such as Layerswap, Orbiter, Rhino.fi, Meson, Owlto, Symbiosis, or Relay.

Manta Bridge FAQ

What network does Manta Bridge use?

The official Manta Pacific bridge connects Ethereum Mainnet and Manta Pacific Mainnet. Manta's site also links to third-party bridge providers for more routes. Users should verify the source chain, destination chain, and asset in the app because Manta Atlantic, Manta Pacific, and third-party routes are separate flows.

Can I bridge USDC to Manta Pacific?

USDC availability depends on the route provider. The official bridge page may default to ETH, while third-party bridges can support stablecoins or swaps into Manta Pacific assets. Users should select USDC in the live bridge interface and confirm the received token contract before sending funds.

Why does Manta Bridge ask to add MetaMask chain?

Wallets need the Manta Pacific network configuration to display balances and sign destination-chain transactions. Adding the chain does not move funds by itself; it only lets the wallet recognize Manta Pacific. Users should add the network from the official bridge or verified documentation, not from random popups.

Is Manta Atlantic the same as Manta Pacific?

No. Manta Pacific is the Ethereum L2 ecosystem route most users mean when searching Manta Bridge. Manta Atlantic is a separate Manta network product, and Manta's official site states that Manta Atlantic will sunset on August 1, 2026. Users migrating Atlantic assets should use the official migration instructions.

How long does Manta Bridge take?

Deposit time depends on Ethereum confirmation, bridge processing, and the selected route. A third-party provider may quote faster settlement than a canonical route, but it introduces provider-specific liquidity and execution assumptions. Users should rely on the live bridge estimate for the selected token and route.

What is the MANTA token used for?

MANTA is the ecosystem token associated with Manta Network. It is separate from ETH deposits through the Manta Pacific bridge. Token uses can include ecosystem incentives, governance, staking or restaking-related programs, and exchange trading, but users should verify current utility through official Manta pages.
